Fehdra's Burden Gauge

------- To install. Copy and paste the text below into zMUD's Command line at the bottom of the window.-------

----- Once installed Right click Gauge to change Colors. The Burden is set to max at 30, you can change that. ------




#CLASS {Panels|Gauges|GaugeBurden}
#VAR Burden {0} {0}
#BUTTON 6 {%mud.burden/30} {} {} {} {%mud.burden} {} {} {Size} {70} {19} {Pos} {0} {423} {71} {} {Gauge||2|30|10|6} {0} "" {Explore|Stack|Top} {} {burden} {4}
#CLASS 0
